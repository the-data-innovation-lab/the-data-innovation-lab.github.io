I am an Assistant Professor of Management at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. My research explores how the increasing availability of big data reshapes the innovation process and strategic decision-making.

My dissertation was recognized as the winner of the Academy of Management's William H. Newman Award (2024) and the INFORMS/Organization Science Dissertation Proposal Competition (2023), and received best paper prizes at the Academy of Management (2024), Strategic Management Society Conference (2023), AIM Conference at USC Marshall (2023), and Wharton Innovation Doctoral Symposium (2023).

For more information, please see my [CV](https://www.matteotranchero.com/pdf/CV_MatteoTranchero.pdf).
